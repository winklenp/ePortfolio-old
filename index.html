About Me

Hello and welcome! My name is Noah Winkler and this is my ePortfolio. I am originally from Louisville, KY, but now reside in Cincinnati, OH. I have three younger siblings, two cats, and a saltwater fish tank which houses a variety of coral, invetabrates and fish. However more than saltwater fish, my true passion is technology. Since the 7th grade, I have been tinkering with and exploring technology. I built my own PC that year, and was instantly hooked on the technological world. Ever since, my passion has only grown. Throughout high school I learned a variety of programming languages such as Java, RobotC, and Python. I co-developed a street-fighter style game with some friends. I lead my schools robotics team as the sole developer while instructing younger students on the work I was doing. I knew that I wanted a career in technology and that a degree was necessary to grow into that goal. I chose the University of Cincinnati for their excellent Computer Science corriculum and generous acceptance of AP credits. While working on my degree, I participated in several co-ops, from developing simulations of autonomous driving algorithms using Python and open source simulation software, to mobile app development for deployment in a local warehouse, to full stack web development. Most recently I have spent my time developing, supporting, managing, and launching fully custom Wordpress sites. I am always open to new experiences, technologies and challenges, and i'm excited to see where my skills take me.
